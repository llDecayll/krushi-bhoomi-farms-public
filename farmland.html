Return On Investment

Daily Milk Income

A Jersey cow produces 20-25 litres of milk a day, earning approximately ₹1,000/day - sold directly through the Karnataka Milk Federation, fully managed by us.

Annual Fruit Income

20-25 fruit-yielding trees per plot produce ~100 kg each per year - worth approximately ₹2,50,000 annually, with returns supported through our local YMCA partnership.

Sandalwood Maturity Value

Each sandalwood tree can sell for up to ₹7,00,000 at 10-15 year maturity - an expected return of up to ₹1.75 Cr per plot considering 8% inflation.
